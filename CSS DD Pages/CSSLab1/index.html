The Ancient Magus'Bride The Golden Yarn


It is many the characters in Kore Yamazaki's The Ancient Magus'Bride that makes its universe so magical. The Golden Yarn is equally inhabited by characters that we were never introduced to in the manga or the anime for that matter.

In Kore Yamazaki's own words,The world of The Ancient Magus' Bride is bigger than Elias and Chise, and many are the folk and spirits who cross their lives' path. Their tales twist together in a taprestry, words like a golden yarn, a weaving of memories and secrets...



This novel is a special addition to the world that fans of The Ancient Magus' Bride are accostomed to. We are introduced to characters like the gancanagh and Alex who are reborn as Defender and Ash, the ultimate tag team and also get to explore Hazel's life beyond his magical mail delivery into his identity as a centaur.
What makes The Golden Yarn all the more especial is that it is the product of the contribution of numerous novelists including Kore Yamazaki herself.
