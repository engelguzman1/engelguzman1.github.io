My Purpose

For this project I chose to base it on The Ancient Magus's Bride because ever since I discovered it, I was fascinated by every aspect of this series. The characters, magic tehniques, soundtracks and the visual elements of the anime drew me to such extend that I even purchased its novels and I plan to read the manga.

I recommend it to anyone who likes magic and folklore while also having wholesome characters with an elaborate and resilient background story.

Some of my interests are:

  • Magic
  • Anime
  • Folklore
  • Writing & reading
  • Taking pictures
